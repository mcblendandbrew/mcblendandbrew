About Us

Music City Blend & Brew is a coffee cart created by two coffee enthusiasts who love Nashville, Seattle, and brewing coffee. They started making coffee in their home kitchen and eventually turned it into a business. They believe that coffee is more than just a beverage; it's a way to connect with others and share stories. They invite you to join them on their journey and let them brew you a cup of coffee.

Services

Find below the services we offer. But we are willing to try something special for you and discuss your idea for your special event

Espresso Symphony

A Full Espresso Bar Experience

Indulge in a symphony of flavors, from velvety lattes to refreshing cold brew. Our menu caters to every coffee aficionado's palate, featuring signature chai lattes, comforting hot chocolate, and a range of standard syrups and alternative milks—all at no extra cost. Elevate your event with our unforgettable coffee experience.

Brew & Bites

Full Espresso Bar with Bakeries

Embark on a culinary adventure where freshly brewed espresso meets delectable pastries. Our Brew & Bites menu presents a harmonious blend of flavors, from classic lattes to innovative specialty drinks. Savor the rich aroma of expertly crafted espresso beverages and indulge in sweet and savory sensations with our local bakery pastries.

Brand in a Cup

The Branded Brew Experience

Elevate your event with our fully customized Brand in a Cup service. Seamlessly integrate your brand into every aspect of our coffee cart, from personalized coffee sleeves to specially crafted blends infused with your brand's essence. Whether it's a corporate gathering, product launch, or grand celebration, our Brand in a Cup service ensures a unique and sophisticated experience that leaves a lasting impression.
